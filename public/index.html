About Us

Since its establishment in 1995, TICT Computers has been a beacon of excellence in computer education and training in Anantapur. With a rich legacy of over three decades in collaboration with the Lions Club Anantapur-Main, we have empowered thousands of students to achieve their dreams by providing top-notch computer training and coaching at nominal fees.

Our training programs have helped countless individuals secure placements in both government and private sectors, paving the way for brighter futures. With a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty, we are committed to delivering the best in education, ensuring our students are equipped with the skills and knowledge required to excel in today's competitive world.

The Director of TICT COMPUTERS - Anantapur is Dr.Y.SOBHAN BABU, With 25+ years of expertise in technology and education, they are committed to providing high-quality learning experiences and helping students achieve their career aspirations.

Students will receive a certification upon successfully completing the course and passing the examinations. These certifications are recognized and add value to your academic and professional profile.
